Research interests
--------------------------------------
I am studying high energy and condensed matter physics using numerical techniques.
I am especially interested in non-perturbative formulation, where physics cannot
be studied with small-parameter expansions or Feynman diagrams.
This includes the use of Monte Carlo methods and tensor networks.
I am also interested in the sign problem.

Currently, I am working on the development and application of Grassmann tensor network on two-dimensional fermionic systems.
